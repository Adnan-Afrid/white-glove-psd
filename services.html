Medicaid Planning & Consulting

We review and process all documentation required to determine your loved one's eligibility for Medicaid—providing expert assistance to ensure approval, while protecting your assets.

Not only can we guarantee you the best outcome with our insight and expert oversight on every detail, but our simple process transforms an otherwise daunting process. Life is already complicated enough—let us handle this tedious task of isolating which mandatory and optional Medicaid benefits you or your loved one can qualify for.

Following your initial phone consultation, we will schedule a meeting with one of our Medicaid experts. After a careful review of all documentation, your White Glove consultant will schedule a meeting to discuss eligibility and advise on next steps in the application process. We will certify that all requirements have been met and after determining that all documentation is accurate and complete, will submit all required paperwork directly on your behalf. After approval, White Glove will continue to meet with you to discuss yearly renewals and resolve any additional requests, as needed.
